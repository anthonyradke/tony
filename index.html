About Me

My name is Anthony Radke and I'm a second-year Mechanical Engineering student at the University of Colorado Boulder. While my academic pursuits fuel my analytical mind, my true passion lies in the realm of visual creativity.

For over six years, I've immersed myself in the world of video editing, motion graphics, and animation, honing my skills through self-teaching and hands-on experience. What started as a hobby has blossomed into a thriving freelance career, where I merge my technical skills with my creativity to bring ideas to life on screen.

I've had the privilege of collaborating with notable figures in the sports industry, including NFL star Juju Smith-Schuster, NBA player Tyler Herro, and many others. These experiences have not only strengthened my portfolio but have also provided insights into the world of athletics and visual storytelling.
